About

Since its 1st event in 2019, PickHacks has become one of the largest hackathons in the Midwest. We hope that you'll join us, and 499 other students, in this unforgettable experience.

FAQ

What

Hackathon

PickHacks is Missouri S&T's annual hackathon â€” an innovation competition where students come together to bring a project to life over the course of a weekend. You come up with the idea, and we provide mentors, resources, and anything else you need. Programming experience isn't necessary; the only requirement is a willingness to learn and have fun! Plus, it's FREE!

When

April, 2021

Pickhacks is taking place the weekend of April 9th through 11th!

Where

Online

All virtual! Once you sign up, you will receive an email with information about how to get into the event. Hopin will be the main platform used, along with Discord

Who

You

Anyone currently enrolled in an educational institution (high school and above) or 12 months out of graduation is able to attend. You don't even have to know how to code to join! Just come in with an open mind and fun ideas to expand on, and you'll be set!


Sidenote: For those who would feel more comfortable knowing how a hackathon flows, we will be offering a prehack event on Saturday, April 3rd from 2-4pm, covering everything there is to know about succeeding at a hackathon. And, if you can't make it, videos will be posted on the Pickhacks Youtube channel afterwards! We hope to see you there!

Theme

Entertainment

This year, our theme is "festival", a homage to the entertainment industry and the communities behind it.

Swag

How to get

Right after you RSVP and are accepted into the event, you can fill out a shipping form. Swag will then be sent out before the event in accordance with your responses, so you can rep the gear while you're hacking away!
