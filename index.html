FAQ

What

Hackathon

PickHacks is Missouri S&T's annual hackathon â€” an innovation competition where students come together to bring a project to life over the course of a weekend. You come up with the idea, and we provide mentors, resources, and anything else you need. Programming experience isn't necessary; the only requirement is a willingness to learn and have fun! Plus, it's FREE!

When

April 3-5, 2020

Friday, April 3rd to Sunday, April 5th, 2020. Opening Ceremony will begin around 6 PM on Friday, and Closing Ceremonies will begin at 3 PM Sunday.

Where

Missouri S&T

Gale Bullman Center, located on the Missouri S&T campus.
*Gale Bullman Multi-Purpose Building*

Who

You

Anyone currently enrolled in an educational institution (high school and above) is able to attend. High school students do not need a chaperone.

Theme

Entertainment

This year, our theme is "festival", a homage to the entertainment industry and the communities behind it. There will be specific prize tracks surrounding this theme, which will be released at the beginning of the event.

Reimburse

Yes

Buses will be provided for nearby schools with seating available on a first-come, first-serve basis. Gas reimbursement will be provided to those who show their receipts. Flights will not be reimbursed this year.
